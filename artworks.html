Chronicles Of Blackletter Poster

This was a poster illustrated to promote the font booklet “Chronicles of Blackletter”. This poster was made using Procreate, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop. The poster is inspired by Marvel movie posters where all superheroes are placed in a sort of collage. The style and color scheme are inspired by Greek mythology as Greek gods and goddesses are featured in the booklet.

Woman in Shackles

This illustration, inspired by the Baroque period, spreads awareness about mental health struggles. The woman symbolizes the modern human, with shackles representing anxiety, depression, and stress. The single teardrop depicts the internalized struggle, and the outstretched hand symbolizes seeking help.
